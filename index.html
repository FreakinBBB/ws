About

The Physician

Navigating the intricate pathways of the developing brain as a Resident in Children Neuropsychiatry at the University of Milan. I've honed my clinical instincts across major Italian pediatric centers—translating textbook neurology into real-world smiles.

AI in Medicine

Because fixing bugs in code is slightly easier than fixing them in human DNA. I'm pursuing a Master's in Explainable AI in Healthcare (University of Pavia) and survived the CCAIM Clinical Summer School in Cambridge. Fluent in Python, R, Machine Learning, and translating geek-speak to doctors.

Research

From decoding epileptic encephalopathies to throwing kids into Virtual Reality (for science and neurorehabilitation, I promise). My research bridges AI diagnostics and clinical genetics, aiming to turn data into better clinical outcomes.
